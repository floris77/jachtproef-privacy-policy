Privacy Policy for JachtProef Alert

Effective Date: October 31, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to JachtProef Alert. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • User preferences (favorite matches and notification settings)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Basic usage data for app functionality
  • Match data from publicly available sources

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Allow you to save favorite matches and notification preferences
  • Sync your preferences across your devices using Apple's CloudKit/iCloud

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your preferences are stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account
  • We do not maintain a separate database of user information
  • All syncing is done through Apple's CloudKit services using your personal iCloud account

5. Third-Party Services

  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • The app scrapes publicly available match data but does not share your interaction with this data
  • We do not use third-party analytics services

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (through the app interface)
  • Delete your data (by deleting the app and associated iCloud data)

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.
