Frequently Asked Questions

How do notifications work?

JachtProef Alert sends you notifications when new matches are available or when their status changes. You can customize which notifications you receive by marking matches with the bell icon.

How do I mark a match as favorite?

Tap the star icon on any match to mark it as a favorite. Favorites are synced across all your devices via iCloud.

How often is match data updated?

Match data is refreshed whenever you open the app and have an internet connection. You can also manually refresh by pulling down on the matches list.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, previously loaded match data is available offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to refresh data and receive new matches.
